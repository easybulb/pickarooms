How to Use Pick-A-Rooms

Welcome! Follow these steps to check in smoothly:

1. Enter Your Phone Number

On the homepage, enter the **phone number** used during your reservation.

2. Retrieve Your PIN

Once submitted, you'll receive a **unique PIN** for door access.

3. Follow the Check-In Instructions

You'll be directed to a **check-in guide** with video instructions.

4. Enjoy Your Stay!

If you need help, visit the Contact Us page.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2025

1. Introduction

Pick A Rooms ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information when using our web app.

2. Information We Collect

  • Booking Information: We receive guest details from Booking.com, including your full name, check-in and check-out dates, assigned room, and phone number.
  • Guest Authentication: Your phone number is used only for authentication purposes.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • No payment or credit/debit card information is collected or processed on this web app.
  • We do not collect or store guest addresses, passport details, or any other sensitive personal information.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To facilitate your check-in process and provide room access instructions.
  • To manage and maintain guest reservations.
  • To improve user experience and ensure security.

5. Data Protection

Your information is only accessible to authorized personnel and is securely stored. We do not share your data with third parties.

6. Third-Party Services

As our guest information comes from Booking.com, please review their Privacy Policy for further details on their data handling.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at easybulb@gmail.com.

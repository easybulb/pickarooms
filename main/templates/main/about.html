About PickARooms

Nestled in the quiet neighborhood of Beswick, Manchester, PickARooms is designed for travelers seeking comfort, convenience, and a seamless self-check-in process. Whether you're visiting for business, a football match, or a concert, our modern, well-appointed rooms provide everything you need for a comfortable stay.

🏠 Our Accommodation

  • Spacious Rooms: Each room features a cozy double bed, premium linens, and some come with a private en-suite bathroom.
  • Modern Amenities: Guests enjoy free high-speed Wi-Fi, a fully equipped kitchen, and an inviting living room.
  • Smart Check-In System: Use your phone number to access your PIN and gain entry to your room hassle-free.
  • Prime Location: Just a 5-minute walk to Etihad Stadium and a short drive to Manchester Piccadilly Train Station.

📍 Explore Beswick & Manchester

Guests love the peaceful atmosphere of Beswick while being minutes away from Manchester’s top attractions.

  • ⚽ Etihad Stadium (5 min walk) - Home of Manchester City FC, perfect for match days or stadium tours.
  • 🎤 Co-op Live Arena (5 min walk) – The UK’s largest indoor arena, hosting world-class concerts and events.
  • 🚆 Manchester Piccadilly Station (5 min drive) - Connects you to the rest of the UK.
  • 🎭 Cultural Landmarks: The Manchester Museum, The Whitworth, and historic Victoria Baths.
  • 🍽️ Dining & Nightlife: New Islington Marina, local pubs, and a variety of restaurants & cafés.
  • 🛍️ Shopping & Leisure: Manchester Arndale, The Co-op, and outdoor escapes like Philips Park.

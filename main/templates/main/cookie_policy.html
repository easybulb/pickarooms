Cookie Policy

Last Updated: February 2025

1. Introduction

Pick-A-Rooms ("we", "our", or "us") uses cookies to enhance your experience and improve the functionality of our web app. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding their use.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help in recognizing returning users, remembering preferences, and analyzing web traffic.

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure the proper functionality of our web app.
  • Remember user preferences, such as login sessions.
  • Analyze how users interact with our site to improve performance.

4. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for basic site functionality (e.g., session management).
  • Performance Cookies: Help us analyze traffic and optimize the user experience.
  • Functional Cookies: Store user preferences to enhance navigation.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We do not use third-party advertising cookies. However, cookies may be used by services integrated with Booking.com or other third-party providers for reservation details.

6. Managing Cookies

You can adjust your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • Delete stored cookies.
  • Block certain types of cookies.
  • Receive notifications when cookies are being used.

Note: Disabling essential cookies may impact the functionality of our web app.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be posted on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Cookie Policy, feel free to contact us at easybulb@gmail.com.
